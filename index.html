If you scramble an incoming craft it sends the craft to another planet which will make those planets rivals too! Hitting a rival drains 30╬ from their ╬ bank account so get those crafts moving! There is no limit to how many actions you can take in a turn but remember that you will always have a full turn to scramble an incoming population craft.
